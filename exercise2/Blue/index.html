BLUE



Inspired by ART NOUVEAU







The purpose of the work is advertisement for a company Apple. The focus of the work is a girl
with long hair that comes out of the apple frame and transforms into expressive curved lines. There are graphic
elements used on the background and in the frame. The frame in Art Nouveau plays a fundamental part in the
composition. Usually the frame is a circular shape, and is placed in the middle. I obeyed this principle, but instead of a
circular shaped frame, I created an apple shaped frame to represent the logo of Apple. The dominant color of the
painting is blue, and it represents cool expansiveness and openness. Me and Alphonse both used women is our works to
demonstrate female lead and independence.
